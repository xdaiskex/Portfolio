PEOPLE

Principal Investigator

Subith S. Vasu, Ph.D.

subith@ucf.edu

Research Activities

Alternative fuels for propulsion and internal combustion engines, supercritical CO2 power cycles, shock wave physics and chemistry, detonations and explosions, energy conversion processes, laser diagnostics and sensors for combustion and emission, laser spectroscopy, space sensors, chemical kinetics experiments and modeling, non-equilibrium aerothermodynamics, laser ignition, high-altitude balloon measurements, and advanced propulsion engines.

Work History

  • University of Central Florida, Orlando, FL: 2012-present
  • Assistant Professor, Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering Department
  • Sandia National Laboratories, Livermore, CA: 2010-2011
  • Post Doctoral Appointee, Combustion Research Facility
  • Stanford University, Stanford, CA: 2010-2010
  • Post Doctoral Researcher, Mechanical Engineering
