Directions

From the Attractions:

  1. 1. Travel East on I-4 to downtown Orlando and take the Amelia St. exit (83 B).
  2. 2. Go straight to Colonial Drive (State Road 50), then take a right.
  3. 3. We're 1.5 miles ahead on the left side of the street, just past the intersection of Mills Ave. (Highway 17-92).

From Winter Park, Maitland and Points North:

  1. 1. Take 17-92 south to Colonial Drive.
  2. 2. Turn left and proceed 1 block down Colonial Drive.
  3. 3. We're on the left side of the street.

From East Orlando:

  1. 1. Drive west on Colonial Drive to just before the 17-92 intersection.
  2. 2. Look for our sign on the right.

From Points West:

  1. 1. Head east on Colonial Drive to the 17-92 intersection, then look for us one block past the intersection.
  2. 2. We will be on the left side of the street.
