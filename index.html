Welcome

Here at Viet Garden, we offer you a wide selection of various mouthwatering cuisines from both Vietnam and Thailand. Our spatious dinning area combined with a family-friendly atmosphere will provide you with a comfortable and fine dining experience. If you burnt the turkey this year and are searching around town to find an open restaurant, rest assured that Viet Garden will welcome you with open arms. Please feel free to stop by anytime during our hours and experience the best in Vietnamese and Thai cuisine.
